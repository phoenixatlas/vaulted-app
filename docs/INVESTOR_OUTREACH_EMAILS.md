> ~11%.

## 8. FINAL COACHING NOTE

Cold outreach to top-tier UK seed VCs is a **5–8% reply-to-meeting
funnel** in a good market, **2–3%** in a bad one. Don't take a "no"
or a silence personally. You are pattern-matching them (fit fund) at
the same time they're pattern-matching you (fit founder). Speed
compounds — 10 sharp cold emails in a week teaches you more about
your pitch than 10 warm intros over 3 months.

**One final rule:** Never send the same email to more than one partner
at the same fund at once. It's a small industry; they compare inboxes.
Pick your partner, send once, wait for reply-or-silence, then either
follow-up (§3) or move to a different partner with a *rewritten*
first-line reason. Recycled first-lines get you blacklisted.

Now go send it. 🚀
