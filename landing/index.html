Features

Remittance without the middlemen.

Vaulted unifies remittance, self-custody, and encrypted communication in one app. No middlemen. No exit fees. No data harvesting.

🌍
Cross-border remittance

Send GBP, USD, or EUR to 8 countries. Vaulted quotes live FX and auto-picks the cheapest chain (XLM · XRP · USDC L2). Fees from £0.50.

🔑
True self-custody

Private keys are generated on-device and never leave your control. Six chains: BTC, ETH, USDC (Ethereum + Polygon + Base + Arbitrum), SOL, XLM, XRP.

🔒
End-to-end encrypted chat

Conversations are encrypted with TweetNaCl on your device. Even our servers see only ciphertext. Group chats included on Vault Pro.

🎥
Secure video calls

WebRTC-powered calls with end-to-end media encryption. Talk to family in the corridor country before you send.

✍
Multi-signature wallets

Require co-signer approval for large transactions. Add a trusted partner, accountant, or yourself on a second device.

👤
Biometric authentication

Face ID or fingerprint locks every send. Your keys stay encrypted at rest, unlocked only by you.

Security

Defense in depth.

Your keys are generated, encrypted, and stored on your device — never on our servers. We don't ask. We can't see. That's the point.

Every piece of Vaulted is engineered to assume our infrastructure could be compromised tomorrow, and your funds would still be safe.

  • BIP-39 12-word recovery phrases
  • TweetNaCl XChaCha20-Poly1305 encryption
  • Biometric device-level authentication
  • Multi-signature transaction approvals
  • Email-based co-signer verification
  • No password recovery — true non-custody
