Features

Built for sovereignty.

Vaulted unifies the tools you actually need — wallet, chat, calls, and security — into one private app. No middlemen. No data harvesting.

🔑
True Self-Custody

Your private keys are generated on-device and never leave your control. We can't access your wallet — even if we wanted to.

🔒
End-to-End Encrypted Chat

Conversations are encrypted with TweetNaCl on your device. Even our servers see only ciphertext. Group chats included.

🎥
Secure Video Calls

WebRTC-powered calls with end-to-end media encryption. Talk freely, with no advertising business model behind your camera.

Tax-Ready Exports

Security

Defense in depth.

Your keys are generated, encrypted, and stored on your device — never on our servers. We don't ask. We can't see. That's the point.

Every piece of Vaulted is engineered to assume our infrastructure could be compromised tomorrow, and your funds would still be safe.

  • BIP-39 12-word recovery phrases
  • TweetNaCl XChaCha20-Poly1305 encryption
  • Biometric device-level authentication
  • Multi-signature transaction approvals
  • Email-based co-signer verification
  • No password recovery — true non-custody
