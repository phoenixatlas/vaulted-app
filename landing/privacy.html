Privacy Policy

Vaulted is a self-custody crypto wallet operated by PhoenixAtlas Technologies Ltd. We take your privacy seriously — non-custody is our entire model.

1. What we collect

Email address (for login + recovery emails), encrypted message ciphertext (we cannot decrypt it), and basic transaction metadata (timestamps + amounts). We do not have access to your private keys or recovery phrase.

2. What we don't collect

We don't collect: your IP address (in logs we keep only last 24 hours for debugging), your contacts, your location, your camera/microphone outside an active call, or any browsing data outside our app.

3. Third-party processors

We use Stripe (payments), Resend (transactional email), Daily.co (video calls), MongoDB Atlas (database), and Cloudflare (DNS). Each is GDPR-compliant.

4. Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and all server-side data at any time by emailing support@phoenix-atlas.com. Your local device data, including your private keys, remain entirely under your control.

5. Contact
